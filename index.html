About Me

My name is Steve and I'm originally from the D.C. area and now living in Florida for the past 19 years. I recently left my long-time job of 18 years at a property insurance company where I eventually became chief catastrophe modeler. In that job, I did manage to code...I created an Excel add-in catastrophe modeling interface tool using C# and a vendor's API. Earlier during that job, I had an 8-year side hustle as the backend PHP developer for my friend's one-person (then two- with me) web development business.

Throughout all of this I've been self-taught. I love and am driven by the creativity and accomplishment involved with building programmatic solutions to users' needs. Also, It goes without saying that I've been standing on the shoulders of giants by taking advantage of free and open source online coding resources graciously offered to the world by others' hard work. Now, through Coding Temple, I've chosen to expand and polish my programming skills.

My hobbies include a passion for indie music, tennis, hiking, biking, beach walking, traveling, watching (and dreaming about doing) local surfing and local skimboarding.
